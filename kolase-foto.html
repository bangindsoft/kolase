ZOOM 100%

Click an empty box to upload a photo. On a photo that's already added: drag to reposition it, scroll the mouse wheel (on desktop) or pinch with two fingers (on mobile) directly over the photo to zoom in/out. A single click on a photo also reveals a zoom slider as an alternative. Everything runs in your browser — photos are never uploaded to any server.
